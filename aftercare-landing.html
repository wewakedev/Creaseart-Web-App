FAQs

What is Aftercare?

Aftercare is a laundry and dry cleaning platform that brings your favourite laundry and dry cleaning merchants closer to you. It’s a one-stop platform designed for your comfort. It empowers you to provide the best laundry and dry cleaning services in your pocket, right when you need them, right where you are. It helps you organise everything with ease. We are your trusted companions, helping you simplify life so you can concentrate on what truly matters: enjoying the moments to the fullest with those who matter to you.

How do I use Aftercare?

Here are six simple steps to use Aftercare:

  • Download our Aftercare App to order services from your favourite laundry and dry cleaning merchant.
  • Enter your pin code in the “vendor finder” section to find your favourite laundry and dry cleaning vendor.
  • Put your favourite laundry and dry cleaning services in your cart.
  • Select your delivery address, and schedule your pickup & delivery date and time.
  • Apply coupons to avail good value discounts on your selected services. You’ll be notified by email when your order is placed.
  • So sit back and relax—no more laundry and dry cleaning errands to run.
Why don’t I see my favourite laundry and dry cleaning merchant on Aftercare?

If the merchant shop is closed when you do your search, it will display at the bottom of the page with the next possible delivery time marked in red. However, if your favourite laundry and dry-cleaning merchant isn't currently included on our platform, we're in progress to get them onboarded.

What are the different payment options available at the time of ordering?

You can pay through multiple payment options such as debit card, credit card, and UPI. All the payment options are safe and secure. Additionally, your payment details are protected by our secure online payment system.

Can I select the preferred delivery time?

Aftercare allows customers to select the preferred delivery time while placing the order. This helps our customers to choose their convenient time that does not tamper with their regular schedule.

How can I be sure my order was received by the laundry and dry-cleaner merchant?

After you place an order, we will send you an email verifying that we received it. If you choose to get text message alerts, you will receive a text as soon as the merchant verifies your order.

Note: Orders for future pickup or delivery are forwarded to the merchant approximately one hour before the specified pickup or delivery time. If the merchant notifies us at the time of confirmation that they are unable to fulfil your order, we will contact you promptly. It's ideal to supply an accurate phone number for future orders and have your phone available in case we or the merchant need to contact you.

Where do the merchant’s ratings and reviews originate from?

The aftercare community provides all ratings and reviews, and a user can only post a review after making a transaction with that merchant.

Your feedback helps customers make better choices, and it also helps merchants improve their service by providing useful feedback.

What if there's an issue with my order?

The quickest approach to resolve an issue is to contact the merchant directly; we give their phone number in every confirmation email. If it doesn't work, you may contact our Customer Support staff through chat, by emailing at support@aftercareco.com
