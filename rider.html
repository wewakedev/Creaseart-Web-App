FAQs

How does Creaseart's delivery system work?

Millions of people seek Creaseart's clean laundry. When clients make an order, we distribute it to riders who earn money by picking up and delivering them. Earn money making deliveries.

Are Aftercare drivers required to have any previous driving experience?

Nope! No prior knowledge is required.But, if you do have prior experience in the rideshare, food, courier service industries, delivering with Creaseart is a great way to earn money.

Riders from other commercial services, such as Uber, ola, Zomato, Swiggy, and Amazon, are welcome to use our service. Catering drivers, taxi drivers, and other workers from all walks of life use the service.

What things do I need to become a Aftercare driver?

To become a Aftercare driver, you must be 18 years old or above, need a driving licence, registration certificate, aadhar card as well as to purchase the creaseart starter kit. Additionally, all you need to make money is a smartphone and a mode of transportation such as a scooter or a bicycle. Everything else will be handled by us.

What are the login hours?

You can work part time or full time. Login hours for Part Time jobs are 5 hours per day and Full Time jobs are 10 hours per day.

How much can I earn?

You don’t need to worry about your expenses. If you’re 18-25, you can make between Rs.14K and Rs.30K part time and full time.

Are there any perks?

Perks await our most hardworking drivers. The perks are in many ways according to your work behaviour.

Do we have to pay any fees to get on boarded as a Aftercare rider?

No, there is no extra fee to become a Aftercare rider. In addition, you only need to buy a starter kit from us.

How will I get paid?

Your payment will be deposited once a month into the bank account you specify.
